About Our Approach

Our model combines multi-source data affecting stock prices and applies sentiment analysis, swarm intelligence algorithm, and deep learning to build the MS-SSA-LSTM model.

  • Establish a unique sentiment dictionary from forum data
  • Optimize LSTM hyperparameters with Sparrow Search Algorithm
  • Integrate sentiment index with fundamental trading data
  • Predict stock prices with high accuracy using LSTM neural networks

Our experiments demonstrate that the MS-SSA-LSTM model outperforms others with high universal applicability, improving R² by 10.74% on average compared to standard LSTM models.
